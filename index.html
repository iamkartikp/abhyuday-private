ABHYUDAY

About Abhyuday

Abhyuday is an inter-collegiate technical fest organized by the Department of Master of Computer Applications at Ramaiah Institute of Technology, which serves as an excellent platform for students for various colleges to display their talents and ideas. Abhyuday 6.0, conducted last year was a grand success. There were participants from various colleges all over Bengaluru. This year, we bring back Abhyuday 7.0, the seventh edition of the fest, with a string of 10 competitions and exciting awards and cash prizes to be won, Abhyuday 7.0 is going to be two days of absolute fun!
